Academy

We have developed a cross-area and company-specific training programme to help employees expand their personal and professional competences in a way that enables them to do their job with love and self-confidence. From product and technical trainings to soft skills, team development and leadership trainings, the GISHNU Academy offers each of our employees the opportunity to learn and apply new methods and tools, to recognise and overcome their own weaknesses, to build knowledge and unleash sleeping potential.

Apprenticeships
